PawPaw

The pawpaw (Asimina triloba) is a deciduous tree native to the eastern United States and Canada. It produces the largest edible fruit native to North America, which has a custard-like texture and a flavor reminiscent of banana, mango, and melon. Learn more

  1. Native Range: Eastern United States and Canada
  2. Fruit Size: Largest edible fruit native to North America
  3. Flavor Profile: Banana, mango, and melon
  4. Tree Height: Up to 35 feet (10.5 meters)
  5. Leaves: Large, simple, and oblong
